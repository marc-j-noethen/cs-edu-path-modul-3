# 🖥️ Free Advice - Select an Azure Advisor recommendation and explain why

**Course:** Cloud & Enterprise Security 2 - Governance & Monitoring | **Date:** 23 April 2026

---

## Task

**Objective:** Explore Azure Advisor in the portal, select a recommendation and explain its benefits in your own words.

**Requirements:**
- Open the Azure Advisor dashboard and review categories such as cost and security.
- Select exactly one recommendation.
- Submit a screenshot of the selected recommendation along with your own justification.

---

## Solution

### Portal steps

```text
1. Search for "Advisor" in the Azure portal and open the service.
2. View the "Overview" dashboard.
3. Go through the available categories such as Cost, Security, Reliability or Performance.
4. Select a recommendation and open the details view.
5. Explain in your own words why the recommendation is useful.
6. Take a screenshot of the recommendation and attach the explanation.
```

## Questions & Answers

**Q:** Why is the selected recommendation a good one?

**A:**
- It highlights a specific vulnerability or opportunity for optimisation in the subscription.
- It helps either to reduce costs, increase the level of security or improve stability.
- It prioritises actions that could easily be overlooked without Advisor.

## Notes

- **Azure Advisor:** Advisor analyses resources and provides personalised recommendations for costs, security, performance and operations.
- **Recommendation readiness:** Newly created resources may take up to 24 hours before Advisor displays recommendations for them.
- **Governance:** Even simple Advisor recommendations can be a good starting point for systematic cloud optimisation.
