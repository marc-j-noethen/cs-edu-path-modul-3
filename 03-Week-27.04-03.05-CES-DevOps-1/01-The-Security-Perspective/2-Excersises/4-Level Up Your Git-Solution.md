**Course:** CES DevOps 1 – The Security Perspective | **Date:** 27 April 2026

## Task

**Objective:** Successfully complete the required levels in LearnGitBranching and confidently apply advanced Git concepts.

**Requirements:**
- Open the website `https://learngitbranching.js.org` and use `levels` to view all available sections.
- In the `A Mixed Bag` section, complete the `Fetch Just One Commit` challenge from Level 1 to 5.
- In the `Advanced Topics` section, complete the `Rebase Over 9000 Times` challenge from Level 1 to 3.
- At the end, check that all the required level buttons are green.

## Solution

### Steps in the game

```text
1. Open the website and enter `levels` in the built-in terminal.
2. Go to the "A Mixed Bag" section and complete the five levels of "Fetch Just One Commit" one after the other.
3. Then switch to the "Advanced Topics" section and complete the three levels of "Rebase Over 9000 Times".
4. If you get stuck, use `hint`; you can use `undo` and `reset` to undo moves or reset the entire level.
5. The task is complete once both required sections are marked entirely green.
```
