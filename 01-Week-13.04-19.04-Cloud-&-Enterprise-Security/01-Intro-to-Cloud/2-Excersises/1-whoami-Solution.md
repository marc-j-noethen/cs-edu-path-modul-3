# 🖥️ whoami… Cloud Edition - Checking your subscription and role in the Azure portal

**Course:** Cloud & Enterprise Security - Intro to Cloud | **Date:** 13 April 2026

---

## Task

**Objective:** Open the subscription in use in the Azure Portal and determine the subscription name and your own role within it.

**Requirements:**
- Log in to the Azure Portal using the student login details provided.
- Open the `Subscriptions` service via the global search bar.
- Note down the subscription name and assigned role, and submit a screenshot containing both pieces of information.

---

## Solution

### Portal steps

```text
1. Open https://portal.azure.com/ in your browser.
2. Log in with your student account.
3. Search for "Subscriptions" in the top search bar.
4. Open the "Subscriptions" service and select the active subscription.
5. On the overview page, note down the name of the subscription and your own role.
6. Take a screenshot showing both pieces of information.
```

## Questions & Answers

**Q:** What is your role in this subscription? What is the name of the subscription?

**A:** 
- The role is shown in the details of the selected subscription.
- The subscription name is shown in the subscription header or overview.
- Both details must be visible together in the screenshot.

---

## Notes

- **Azure Portal:** The portal is the graphical management interface for Azure resources and subscriptions.
- **Subscription:** A subscription is the central container for billing, permissions and resources.
- **Role (RBAC):** The role defines which actions a user is permitted to perform within a subscription.
